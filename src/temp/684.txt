DBWorld Message Accepting applications to the ACM Grace Murray Hopper Award (deadline Jan 15).
Refer to https://awards.acm.org/hopper for more details.

The ACM GMH Award is for outstanding young computer scientists, selected on the basis of a single recent major technical or service contribution. The candidate must have been 35 years of age or less at the time the qualifying contribution was made.
